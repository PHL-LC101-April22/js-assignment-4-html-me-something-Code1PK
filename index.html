5 Family Beaches in Pennsylvania I wish to visit!

«My family loves to spend time on the beach, splash in the water, and lay on the sand. I have two toddlers and we need places with shallow waters, a well-maintained beach, and lifeguards. So, I found some beautiful beaches which we want to visit this summer.»

1.Beltzville State Park Beach

Featuring 525 feet of sandy shorelines, Beltzville State Park Beach is one of the nicest family-friendly beaches in Pennsylvania offering a wide array of fun activities for the whole family. Beltzville State Park Beach is a great place for you and your loved ones to enjoy water activities like boating, kayaking, water skiing, and paddle boating. If you don’t feel like doing much on your vacation, the soft sandy shoreline is perfect for spending the day relaxing and sunbathing.

2.Kohler Beach

Kohler beach, one of the eleven beaches at Presque Isle State Park, is the calmest and most sheltered beach of them all, and one of the top toddler-friendly beaches in Pennsylvania. This beach features a broad and flat shoreline, gentle waters with little to no waves, and alert lifeguards who are always on the ready. Kohler Beach has some of the most shallow waters in Pennsylvania, and there is no sudden drop in the water, making it perfect for your toddlers. This coastline also has nearby playgrounds that are the perfect size for your children.

3.Gouldsboro State Park Beach

Offering a classic beach experience for beachgoers, this simplistic coastline is one of the best family beaches in Pennsylvania. Gouldsboro State Park Beach is a wonderful place for you to unwind and relax. Rent a boat, kayak, or paddleboard and wade the waters, relax on the beach and get a tan, or help your tykes make sculptures out of the sand. Bring a ball or frisbee and play fun games with your loved ones, or enjoy a game of tag. Teach your youngsters how to catch a line, or splash around in the cool waters.

4.Mt. Gretna Lake and Beach

Mt. Gretna Lake and Beach, one of the most beautiful beaches in Pennsylvania, features 8 acres of sandy shorelines and a well-maintained lawn. This is also one of the best beaches in Pennsylvania for kids because it features a separate children’s dock that has much shallower water and away from all the extreme activities. To make your family vacations even more unforgettable, Mt. Gretna Lake and Beach has water tubes, kayaks, and canoes for rent. These are affordable and will allow you to have a more enjoyable time in the water.

5.Mauch Chunk Lake Park Beach

Mauch Chunk Lake Park Beach is a very popular beach in Pennsylvania. It features breathtaking views of Flagstaff Mountain. Mauch Chunk Lake Park Beach is the ideal location for families who enjoy camping and want to go camping on their beach vacation. The beach features attentive lifeguards who keep a close eye on everything, a sandy shoreline well-suited for your kids to play in, and pristine waters.
